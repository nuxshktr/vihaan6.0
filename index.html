VIHAAN 6.0
presented by IEEE DTU

Vihaan 6.0, the 6th Edition of IEEE DTU's Annual Flagship Hackathon, looks toward continuing the legacy of Delhi's largest hackathon. And we bring to you the most awaited 24-hour hackathon where the participants will get to tread on the path of innovation and find solutions to some real-world pressing problems, ranging from healthcare to fintech and everything in between. The participants will get to present solutions to a jury of experienced industry professionals, opening up opportunities to network and collaborate. Of all, the exposure to the community of tech enthusiasts that you'll get around here, shall sure be unmatched.

Why you should Join Event ?

Build Innovative Solutions

Innovative mindset has got till here, innovation will take you ahead. In the hackathon, by working with a team of like-minded individuals, you can brainstorm new ideas, develop innovative solutions to problems, and push the boundaries of what is possible.

Improve Coding Skills

Hackathons provide an excellent opportunity to improve your coding skills. By working on challenging projects with tight deadlines, you can hone your coding skills and problem-solving abilities, learn new programming languages, and gain experience working with different tools and technologies.

Networking

Participating in a hackathon can help you build your network. You'll get the chance to meet other developers, designers, and entrepreneurs who are passionate about technology and innovation. New opportunities, partnerships, and collaborations are waiting for you down the road.

Win Prizes

Prizes provide the motivation to strive for more and do better. Prizes can range from cash to tech gadgets and even job offers. If you're looking to jumpstart your career in tech, winning a hackathon can be a great way to get noticed.

Collaboration

Hackathons foster collaboration among participants. You'll have the chance to work with people from different backgrounds and skillsets, which can lead to new insights and ideas. Additionally, working on a team can help you develop your communication and teamwork skills, which are valuable in any industry.

Fun

Last but not the least, participating in a hackathon can be a lot of fun! You'll have the opportunity to explore and work on exciting projects, meet new people, and learn new things. Whether you're a seasoned developer or a novice, there's something in store for everyone at a hackathon! Forget not to come and explore.

tracks

Tracks

Education20

This track focuses on developing innovative solutions to address the challenges faced by the education system due to the COVID-19 pandemic, including access to education, digital literacy, personalized learning, teacher training, and mental health concerns. Solutions should be feasible, scalable, sustainable, and culturally sensitive.

Fintech20

The G20 summit is focusing on Fintech, which has the potential to transform the financial landscape and drive global economic growth. This track aims to address financial inclusion, regulatory compliance, digital payments, financial stability, and green finance through innovative fintech solutions.

Sustainability20

The G20 is committed to promoting economic growth while ensuring environmental sustainability and social inclusion. This track aims to identify innovative solutions that address challenges related to sustainable transportation, agriculture, clean energy, sustainable consumption, and waste reduction, with criteria including innovation, feasibility, and impact.

Tourism20

This track offers a chance for participants to address challenges facing India's tourism industry before the G20 summit in 2023. Participants can tackle problems related to language barriers, digitalization, immersive experiences, sustainable tourism, and MSME development.

Rozgaar20

This track focuses on addressing employment challenges in India through innovative solutions in job matching, upskilling, remote work, gig economy, and entrepreneurship/small businesses. The track aims to promote entrepreneurship, innovation, and sustainable job opportunities in India.

Culture20

This track focuses on celebrating and preserving India's diverse cultural heritage through innovative solutions, such as virtual cultural experiences, language learning, cultural preservation, and community building.

Healthcare20

This track aims to address challenges in the Indian healthcare sector, including shortage of trained staff, inadequate funding and infrastructure, and obstacles to implementing virtual healthcare and AI. Solutions are sought to improve healthcare accessibility, affordability and quality.

Open Innovation

The Open Innovation track aims to provide a platform for young engineers to work on their creative ideas and solve real-world industrial problems. The focus areas include autotech, consumer tech, automation in hazardous jobs, and sports tech.

questions

FAQ's

What is Vihaan?
+ -

Vihaan is a national level hackathon to challenge your creative potential and build innovative solutions to existing problems.

Hackathon? What's that?
+ -

In general, It is a 2-3 days long competition which makes you come up with a technical solution to an existing problem under the topics provided. The purpose of a hackathon is for a group of programmers to work together on a collaboration project. In a hackathon, you have to code during the event although you can come up with ideas for how to proceed beforehand.

Wait so am I required to know coding? I don't know if I'm skilled enough.
+ -

Participation is the essential part, learning is the goal. You might need a basic idea of coding though on the development side.

Am I required to pay anything?
+ -

No, it's totally free. We only require your knowledge and enthusiasm.

I'm interested, How do I register?
+ -

Registration links are active on the website. Registration is free of cost.

What's the prize if you end up winning? Give me some Incentive.
+ -

There is a butt load of a cash prize and swags up for grabs. Further details will be released soon. Although, last year the cash prize were around 1.5 lakhs and swags worth 5 lakhs of rupees.

Are there any prerequisites for participating? Am I required to have any form of tools?
+ -

You'll just need your trusty laptop and a sufficient bandwidth internet connection.

Is it a team competition, can't I participate alone?
+ -

Yes, you can participate alone. Nevertheless teaming up will help you socialize and increase your reach.

So what's the participation criteria for me and my teammates?
+ -

There is only one, you must be at least above 13 and are a college undergraduate or a high school student.

That's great! Any other major thing I need to know?
+ -

Nope, you're all set. Rev your engines up!

Don't miss out on the chance to showcase your skills and make an impact.
